Support

Need help with Posture? Email posture.app.support@gmail.com and you'll get a reply.

Manage or cancel your subscription

Posture Premium is billed by Apple. To view, change, or cancel it, open iOS Settings > your name (Apple ID) > Subscriptions, choose Posture, and pick a plan or cancel. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep access until the end of the paid period. Refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Delete your data

Your data lives only on your device. To remove it, open Settings inside the app and use the delete-your-data control, which erases your completed-day history, goals, and posture screenings. Deleting the app also removes all of its on-device data.

Privacy

Posture has no account and no server. The camera screening is analyzed on your phone and never uploaded. Full detail is in the Privacy Policy.

Common questions

Is the posture screening medical advice?

No. Posture is a general-wellness app. A screening produces estimates shown with a confidence range; it is not a diagnosis and does not replace a qualified professional.

Do I need to pay to use it?

No. You can screen your posture and follow a corrective routine on the free tier. Posture Premium is optional.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The screening and routines run entirely on your device and do not need a connection.

Something isn't working.

Email posture.app.support@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version and a description of the problem.
