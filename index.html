How it works

Screen, then practice

📷

1. Screen

Stand in front of your phone. The camera estimates your posture from a few angles and shows the results with an honest confidence range.

🧐

2. Get a plan

Your screening turns into a short corrective routine that targets what it actually found, not a generic list.

📈

3. Track change

Re-screen over time and watch the trend. Honest minimum-change bands mean noise is never shown as progress.

Privacy

Turn on airplane mode. The scan still works.

There is no account, no sign-up, and no server that receives your data. The camera image is analyzed on your phone and is never saved or uploaded. The app stores only the resulting posture estimates, on your device.

No account

No email, no sign-up, no profile to create. Open the app and go.

Photos never leave

The scan image is processed in memory and discarded. It is never written to disk or sent anywhere.

No tracking

No analytics SDKs, no third-party code, no data collection of any kind.

Honest by design

A real free tier, not a paywall

You can screen your posture and follow a corrective routine without paying. Posture Premium adds more, but the core is genuinely usable for free. No dark patterns, no fake countdowns, no forced trial.
