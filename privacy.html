Privacy Policy

App: Posture  ·  Developer: Matteo Manzoni  ·  Contact: posture.app.support@gmail.com
Effective: 29 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 29 June 2026

Summary

Posture is built to collect nothing. There is no account, no sign-up, and no server that receives your data. Everything you do in the app, including any posture screening, is processed and stored on your device.

What we collect

We do not collect personal data. Posture has no user accounts and no analytics SDK. We do not see your name, email, location, contacts, or usage.

Camera and photos

If you use a posture screening, the camera image is analyzed entirely on your device to produce posture estimates. Your images are never uploaded and never leave your phone. Posture does not save your photos to your photo library unless you explicitly choose to, and does not transmit them anywhere.

Data stored on your device

Your routine completions, streak, selected goals, generated plan, and preferences are stored locally on your device (in the app's storage and standard iOS settings). This data stays on the device, is included in your own encrypted device backups if you use them, and is removed when you delete the app.

Subscriptions

Posture Premium is sold as an in-app subscription handled by Apple. Apple processes the purchase and manages billing and renewals; we never receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We receive only the anonymous subscription status needed to unlock premium features on your device.

Third parties

Posture does not share data with third parties, because it does not collect data. The only third party involved is Apple, solely for processing your optional subscription and app delivery.

Children

Posture is intended for people 18 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Your choices

  • You can use the full free tier without any purchase.
  • You can manage or cancel a subscription anytime in iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
  • You can remove all on-device data from within the app, or by deleting the app.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and post the new version at the policy URL. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.

Contact

Questions about privacy: posture.app.support@gmail.com.

Not medical advice. Posture is a general-wellness app and is not a medical service.
